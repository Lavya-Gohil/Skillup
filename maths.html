Maths Course Class 10-12

Chapter 1: Algebra

Understand the basics of algebra, including variables, equations, and functions.

Topics Covered

  • Variables
  • Equations
  • Functions

Variables

Variables are symbols used to represent numbers. Example:

x + 2 = 5

Equations

Equations are mathematical statements that show the equality of two expressions. Example:

2x + 3 = 7

Functions

Functions relate inputs to outputs. Example:

f(x) = 2x + 1

Chapter 2: Geometry

Learn about different shapes, their properties, and how to calculate their area and perimeter.

Topics Covered

  • Triangles
  • Circles
  • Polygons

Triangles

Triangles have three sides and three angles. The sum of the angles is always 180 degrees. Example:

Area of a triangle = 0.5 * base * height

Circles

Circles are shapes with all points equidistant from the center. Example:

Circumference = 2 * π * radius

Chapter 3: Trigonometry

Discover the relationships between the angles and sides of triangles.

Topics Covered

  • Sine, Cosine, Tangent
  • Pythagorean Theorem

Sine, Cosine, Tangent

These functions relate the angles of a triangle to the lengths of its sides. Example:

sin(θ) = opposite / hypotenuse
cos(θ) = adjecent / hypotenuse
tan(θ) = opposite / adjecent

Pythagorean Theorem

The Pythagorean theorem relates the lengths of the sides of a right triangle. Example:

a² + b² = c²
